SCOIL GHEIMHRIDH GHAOTH DOBHAIR, 2017 TAKES PLACE FROM 27 DECEMBER 2017 UNTIL 1st JANUARY 2018

BREIS EOLAIS: info@localhost/scoil-g || +353 (0)74 956 0679

 
The fourth Scoil Gheimhridh Ghaoth Dobhair will take place from Wednesday 27th December 2017 until Monday 1st January 2018. This year’s programme boasts a wide range of events, including traditional Irish music instrumental, song, Irish language classes; lectures; music recitals; kids’ events; and a series of brilliant concerts featuring the finest of musicians. All the events will take place in Gaoth Dobhair, Co. Donegal, and will be held in seven principal venues: Teach Jack, Na Doirí Beaga; Teach Hiúdaí Beag, An Bun Beag; Pobalscoil Ghaoth Dobhair, Na Doirí Beaga; and An Gailearaí, Údarás Business Park, Gaoth Dobhair, and Club CLG, Gaoth Dobhair; Óstán, An Chúirt, Gaoth Dobhair and Ionad Cois Locha in Dún Lúiche Enjoy!
